About

Welcome to ANPR

Revolutionizing traffic and security in Pakistan with smart technology! Imagine speeding up toll-booth access, boosting security at entry points, and streamlining traffic flow – all thanks to Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) for Pakistani license plates. This innovative project uses AI and image processing to identify vehicles instantly, opening doors to a safer, more efficient future for everyone on the road. With cutting-edge algorithms trained on local plate formats, this ANPR system promises accuracy and performance tailored to Pakistan's unique needs.

This project harnesses the power of computer vision and deep learning to accurately read even the most challenging license plates, from faded text to custom lettering. By tackling local variations and complexities, this ANPR solution promises robust performance for improved traffic management and security.
